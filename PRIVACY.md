# Privacy Policy

**iospharo** is an open source Pharo Smalltalk VM for iOS and macOS.

## Data Collection

This app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Network Usage

The app itself makes no network connections. However, the Pharo environment running inside the VM may make network requests (e.g., downloading packages from the Pharo catalog) at your direction. These connections are initiated by the Pharo image, not by the app, and no user data is sent.

## Local Storage

All data (Pharo images, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device in the app's private container. This data is not accessible to other apps and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Analytics

This app does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/avwohl/iospharo/issues

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be posted to this repository.
