# Pharo Education Contacts

Institutions and people teaching with Pharo Smalltalk — potential
audience for iospharo when it ships on the App Store.

  Pharo-users mailing list (educators + practitioners)
    https://lists.pharo.org/list/pharo-users.lists.pharo.org

  Pharo Discord (~1200 members)
    https://discord.gg/QewZMZa

  Pharo Consortium board
    board@pharo.org

  ESUG board (European Smalltalk User Group)
    board@esug.org

  FAST (Fundacion Argentina de Smalltalk — Latin American network)
    info@fast.org.ar
    https://www.fast.org.ar

## Conferences and Events

  ESUG 2026 — 31st European Smalltalk User Group Conference
    July 4-10, 2026, Plovdiv, Bulgaria
    Call for presentations includes "Educational material" and "Teaching pearls"
    Submission deadline: May 1, 2026
    https://esug.org

  isLoop Summer School — free, beginner-friendly, organized by Pharo Consortium
    Runs ~twice per year (recent: Montpellier, Split)
    https://isloop.pharo.org

  Smalltalks — annual free conference organized by FAST
    Focus on Latin American Smalltalk/Pharo community
    https://www.fast.org.ar


## Free Educational Resources (good context for outreach)

  MOOCs
    "Live Object Programming in Pharo" (beginner, 7 weeks)
      https://mooc.pharo.org
    "Advanced Object-Oriented Design with Pharo" (intermediate)
      https://advanced-design-mooc.pharo.org
    Lecture series
      https://lectures.pharo.org

  Textbooks (all free, open-source)
    https://books.pharo.org
    "Pharo by Example 9", "Deep into Pharo",
    "Learning OOP, Design and TDD with Pharo", etc.

  Exercism track (50 exercises with mentoring)
    https://exercism.org/tracks/pharo-smalltalk
